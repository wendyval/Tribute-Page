Selena Quintanilla

The "Queen of Tejano" music

Here is a short time line of Selena Quintanilla's life:

  • 1971 - Born in Lake Jackson, Texas
  • 1980 - Selena and her family move to Corpus Christi, Texas
  • 1981 - The group Selena y Los Dinos is formed
  • 1984 - Selena y Los Dinos record their first LP record
  • 1987 - Selena wins the Female Vocalist of the Year
  • 1989 - Selena releases her self-titled album
  • 1990 - Selena and Chrsitopher Perez began dating
  • 1992 - Selena and Chris get married
  • 1994 - Selena opened two boutiques called Selena Etc. when she started her own clothing line.
  • 1995 - Murdered at the age of 23 in Corpus Christi, Texas
